# Public documents related to Cleveland Scene's piece on Flock Safety 

Despite its population having voted to put protections for abortion into the state constitution, Ohio has a contract with Flock Group Inc., a company that has been accused of helping Texas authorities track a woman who fled the Lone Star State to get away from its draconian forced-birth laws. 

See https://www.clevescene.com/news/ohio-state-highway-patrol-has-contract-with-flock-firm-that-tracked-abortion-patient/ for the article that resulted from these documents.
